HAMR Time

Train for the HAMR. Own Your Score.

App Store (Coming Soon) Contact

HAMR Time is the first app built specifically for Air Force Airmen to simulate, score, and train for the 20-meter HAMR shuttle run on the PFRA. Available for iPhone and Apple Watch.

Features

Test Simulator — Run a full HAMR simulation with accurate beep timing across all 21 levels, just like the real test.

Score Calculator — Enter your shuttle count, age group, and gender to see your exact PFRA score. Reverse-lookup tells you the shuttles needed for your target.

Training Programs — Structured plans to build cardio endurance progressively. Track workouts and watch your shuttle count climb.

Apple Watch — Real-time haptic alerts, current level and shuttle count at a glance. Focus on the run, not your phone.

Official Scoring — Uses the 2026 finalized PFRA scoring charts for all age groups, genders, and altitude corrections.

History & Trends — Save every practice result and track improvement over time.
