HAMR Time

Train for the HAMR. Own Your Score.

The first app built specifically for Air Force Airmen to simulate, score, and train for the 20-meter HAMR shuttle run on the PFRA.

Coming Soon to the App Store

Built for Airmen, by Airmen

Everything you need to prepare for the HAMR and know your score before test day.

⏱

Test Simulator

Run a full HAMR simulation with accurate beep timing across all 21 levels. Warm up, pace yourself, and finish on your terms — just like the real test.

★

Instant Score Calculator

Enter your shuttle count, age group, and gender to see your exact PFRA score. Reverse-lookup mode tells you the shuttles needed for your target points.

⚡

Training Programs

Structured training plans to build your cardio endurance progressively. Track workouts, log progress, and watch your shuttle count climb.

⌛

Apple Watch Companion

Get real-time haptic alerts as beeps approach, see your current level and shuttle count at a glance, and keep your focus on the run — not your phone.

🎯

Official PFRA Scoring

Uses the 2026 finalized PFRA scoring charts for all age groups and genders, including altitude correction factors.

📊

Test History & Trends

Save every practice result and track your improvement over time. Know exactly where you stand heading into your next assessment.
