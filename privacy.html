Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

HAMR Time is built with your privacy in mind. We keep it simple: we do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data.

Data Collection

HAMR Time does not collect any user data. Your profile settings, test results, training history, and scores are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to us or any third party.

Analytics & Tracking

HAMR Time does not use any analytics services, tracking pixels, or advertising frameworks. We do not track how you use the app.

Third-Party Services

HAMR Time does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. The app functions entirely on your device.

Contact Form

If you choose to contact us through the support form on our website, the name, email, and message you provide are sent to us via Formspree so we can respond to your inquiry. We do not use this information for any other purpose.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us through the contact form on our website.
